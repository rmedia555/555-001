Services

Planting and Cultivation

Bazhong Fannong Supply and Marketing Agricultural Comprehensive Service Co., Ltd. specializes in providing comprehensive planting and cultivation solutions. From Chinese herbal medicine to grains, fruits, vegetables, and more, we offer expert guidance and support for all stages of crop growth. Our services encompass a wide range of planting activities, including tea, nut, and spice crop cultivation, as well as management of sugar crops and trees. With our dedication to sustainable agriculture and advanced techniques, we help farmers maximize their yields and cultivate thriving crops.

Sales and Distribution

At Bazhong Fannong Supply and Marketing Agricultural Comprehensive Service Co., Ltd., we excel in the sales and distribution of agricultural products. From livestock and grains to horticultural products, our extensive network ensures efficient and reliable distribution channels for farmers and producers. Additionally, we facilitate the purchase of real estate Chinese herbal medicines and primary agricultural products, providing seamless transactions and access to quality resources.

Processing and Equipment

Our commitment to enhancing agricultural productivity extends to processing and equipment services. Bazhong Fannong Supply and Marketing Agricultural Comprehensive Service Co., Ltd. offers advanced solutions for the primary processing of both edible and non-edible agricultural products. Moreover, we provide sales and maintenance of agricultural machinery, including professional equipment for various agricultural sectors. With our comprehensive approach, we empower farmers with the tools and technology needed for success in modern agriculture.
